BEST Faculty & Staff

At Shyama Public School, Gyanpur, our faculty and staff are the heart and soul of our institution. We take immense pride in our team of dedicated educators and supportive staff who not only impart knowledge but also inspire, mentor, and guide our students towards achieving excellence. Each member of our team is carefully selected for their expertise, passion, and commitment to nurturing young minds, ensuring that every classroom is a space for innovative learning and holistic development.

Our faculty members bring a wealth of academic experience, modern teaching methodologies, and a genuine enthusiasm for education, creating an engaging and dynamic learning environment. They continuously pursue professional growth and adopt the latest pedagogical strategies, ensuring that every lesson is both meaningful and transformative. Complementing our academic team, our administrative and support staff work diligently behind the scenes to create a welcoming, efficient, and safe environment.

Infrastructure & Facilities

Explore our premium facilities designed to support educational and physical growth.

Modern Classrooms

Our classrooms feature multimedia projectors, ergonomic seating, and innovative design that creates a truly interactive learning experience for every student.

Innovation Spaces

Our innovation hubs and maker spaces offer state-of-the-art environments where eager students explore creative projects, experiment with ideas, and collaborate.

Science Labs

Our advanced science and technology labs, equipped with modern instruments and digital tools, foster hands-on exploration, critical thinking, and innovation.

Computer Labs

Our computer labs, equipped with modern software and fast internet, empower students to enhance digital skills and foster interactive projects.

Transport

Our efficient transport system ensures safe, timely travel with modern, reliable buses and attentive drivers for students, staff, and visitors.

Sports Ground

Our sports and recreational facilities include well-maintained fields, courts, and a modern gymnasium, promoting physical fitness, teamwork, and healthy lifestyles.
