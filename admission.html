Admission Guidelines

We appreciate your interest in Shyama Public School, Gyanpur. Our admission process is designed to be transparent and straightforward. Please review the criteria and submit your inquiry form.

Required Documents

  • Self-attested copy of Student's Birth Certificate.
  • Transfer Certificate (TC) from previous recognized school (if applicable).
  • Report card or marksheet of previous class completed.
  • 4 Passport-size photographs of the student.
  • Aadhar Card copy of Student and Parents.

Eligibility Age Limit

Class Grade Minimum Age
Nursery / L.K.G 3 - 4 Years
Class I 5 - 6 Years
Class VI 10 - 11 Years
